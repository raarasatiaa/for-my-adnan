Your Birthday Letter

My dear Adnan,
Kamu sekarang udah genap 19 tahun, aku yakin akan banyak berdatangan cinta kasih dan keluh kesah di hari-hari yang mendatang.
Tapi yang harus kamu tau, jauh-dekat I'll always be with you till the end of the line because I always feel you whenever I am.
SOo makaci udah kembali dan meyakinkan aku lagi bahwa kasih sayang kamu selalu buat aku.
Terimakaci sudah kembali hadir dan memenuhi tangki cinta aku. Terima kasih juga karena selalu berusaha, aku harap kamu selalu memenangkan peperangan yang sedang atau akan kamu hadapi,
kalau kamu kalah pun I will be on your side—kita rayakan setiap menang dan kalahnya bersama.
Selamat ulang tahun my Adnan.
With infinity love, your Bocil.

Special Poetry from tulisanara

You’ve always reminded me of the sky at dusk—
quiet, constant, and endlessly warm.
There’s a softness in the way you exist,
like the world slows down just to listen
when you speak.

Nineteen.
A number, but also a doorway—
to the years you’ll grow into yourself,
to dreams you’ll chase gently,
and moments you’ll still watch the sunset.

If peace had a heartbeat,
I think it would sound like you.
If warmth had a name,
I think it would whisper yours.

So here’s to you—
to the man who never rushes the day,
who teaches the world how to breathe.
Happiest Birthday for you, My Yellow—
The light that stays even when the sky turns gray.
